PROGRAM "WE" org.

PRIME FOCUS:


To promote a universal declaration for human rights social and economic order, based on truth and    justice.
“The moment a little boy is concerned with which is a jay and which is a sparrow,
he can no longer see the birds or hear them sing.”

To demand virtue & oppose wrongdoing in the exercise of power (from whatever base that power       derives – e.g. political, judicial, media, economic, military, personal, etc).

To gather information about, to publicize, atrocities, oppression, discrimination, & other abuses of    divinely-granted rights.

To campaign to bring the perpetrators & their accomplices to justice.

“and together we will empower humans from“body to mind” enabling humans conquer
their “i” and attain independence of their brain, to lead a positive,
balanced & complete life.”
